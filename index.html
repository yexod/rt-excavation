Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know

What areas does RT Excavation serve?

We're based in Smithfield, Utah and primarily serve Cache County and the surrounding area — including Logan, Hyde Park, North Logan, Providence, Richmond, and Wellsville. Not sure if we cover your location? Just give us a call and we'll figure it out.

Is RT Excavation licensed and insured?

Yes. RT Excavation LLC holds an active Utah contractor license, and Tyler is a certified heavy equipment operator. We take credentials seriously because they protect you as much as they protect us.

Do you take on small residential jobs?

Absolutely — that's our specialty. We focus on residential projects and love working directly with homeowners. No job is too small if it's something we can help with.

How quickly can you schedule a project?

We're typically able to schedule faster than larger companies because we're not juggling a massive backlog. Reach out and Rashelle will get you on the calendar as soon as possible.

How do I get a quote?

Call or text us at (435) 799-1088, or use the contact form on this page. Tell us what you're working with and we'll get back to you fast — usually within a few hours during business hours.

What equipment do you use?

Tyler operates a commercial truck and sources the right heavy equipment for each job. Rather than maintaining a full fleet (which drives up costs), we lease what the project needs — so you're not paying for overhead you don't need.
